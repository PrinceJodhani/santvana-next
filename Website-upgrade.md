# 🟩 **FAQ PAGE**

###  **META TITLE**

**Psychologist FAQs | Therapy & Counselling in Surat**

### **🔹 META DESCRIPTION**

Answers to common questions about therapy, counselling, psychologists vs psychiatrists, fees, sessions & confidentiality at Santvana, Surat.

1. ### **Which is the best psychologist in Surat?**

The best psychologist is one who is professionally trained, experienced, and follows ethical, evidence-based practices. At Santvana Psychological Well-Being Centre, our team includes **clinical psychologists and professionally trained therapists** who provide personalised care based on individual needs.

2. **Should I see a psychiatrist or a psychologist?**  
    If medication is required, a psychiatrist may be consulted. For therapy, counselling, and psychological assessments, a psychologist is recommended. At Santvana, we guide clients and refer to the psychiatrists near me when needed.

3. **Do you have a psychiatrist at Santvana?**  
    We do not have an in-house psychiatrist. However, we coordinate care and referrals with trusted and best psychiatrists in Surat when medication support is required.

4. **When should I seek counseling or therapy?**  
    

You may consider seeking help if you are experiencing persistent sadness, anxiety, changes in sleep patterns, difficulty concentrating, relationship issues, trauma, or any difficulty that interferes with your daily functioning or overall well-being. Anything that is bothering or distressing you is a valid reason to seek support.

5. **Is therapy confidential?**

Absolutely. All therapy sessions are **strictly confidential**, except in situations involving safety concerns, as per ethical guidelines.

6. **How long does a session last?**

 Individual sessions are typically 50 to 60 minutes long, while couple or family sessions might be longer, often 80-90 minutes.

7. **How many sessions will I need?** 

The number of sessions varies depending on the concern and individual goals. Some clients benefit in a few sessions, while others may require longer-term support. On an average 12-15 sessions.

8. **Will the therapist give me advice?**

Therapists typically will not tell you what to do. Instead, they provide a non-judgmental space, ask questions, and help you explore your thoughts, emotions, and behaviors to find your own solutions and develop healthier coping strategies. 

9. ### **What are your fees?**

Our fees vary depending on the **type of service**, **session duration**, and **professional involved**.We aim to keep our services **transparent and affordable**.

For detailed and updated fee information, please **contact us directly via phone, WhatsApp, or during appointment booking**.

10. **Do we have to come through a doctor referral?**  
    No, a doctor’s referral is **not required**. You can directly book an appointment with our therapists or psychologists. If needed, we may suggest or coordinate with a doctor later.

11. ### **Does therapy work?**

Yes, therapy works when it is **regular, goal-focused, and matched to your needs**.  
 It helps people manage emotions, change unhelpful patterns, build coping skills, and improve overall well-being. Results depend on consistency and active participation.

12. ### **Do you provide internships for students?**

Yes. We offer **internship opportunities for psychology and allied field students**, providing **hands-on clinical exposure** under professional supervision.  
 Interns gain practical experience in therapy settings, assessments, observations, and case discussions.

For eligibility and details, please **contact us directly**.
